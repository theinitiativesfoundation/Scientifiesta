Why?

Why should you participate here?

It teaches you how to lose and win. The discouraging truth is that you will probably fail a fair amount of times before you get your first award. It happened to me on a number of occasions because I started going to serious competitions "late" (when I started going on national competitions at the age of 14, most other people already had 2-3 years of experience). I know this may sound discouraging, but initially others are going to seem much better than you, and it's tempting to give up. To conclude, you are going to learn an important lesson: it takes a on of effort to become a winner, but it's totally worth it..
